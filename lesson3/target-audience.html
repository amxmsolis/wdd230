Target Audience and Scenarios

The target audience of the MCCOC.mx website is described below:

Who: entrepreneurs interested in making better business by improving their processes, and networking.

Age: from 18

Income: The scope is very open for the business of all sizes (micro, small, middle, and big companies). Also, startups and mature companies can participate.

Require: Make business in Mexico City.
